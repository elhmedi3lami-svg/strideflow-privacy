Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 10, 2026

StrideFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal productivity application designed to help you manage tasks, habits, calendar events, focus sessions, and notes. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

StrideFlow does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter in the app — including tasks, habits, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device only using Android's SharedPreferences storage.

2. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not use any servers, databases, or cloud services. No data is ever sent to us or any third party.

3. Permissions

StrideFlow may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to send you reminders for tasks and habits you set.
  • Exact Alarm — to deliver reminders at the exact time you specify.
  • Storage / Media — to allow you to attach images or files to your notes.
  • Boot Completed — to restore scheduled reminders after device restart.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit your data.

4. Third-Party Services

StrideFlow does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No SDKs that collect user data are included in the app.

5. Children's Privacy

StrideFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no data collection, and no online features.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
